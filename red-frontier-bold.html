Red Frontier Bold – Stronger Cocktail

Red Frontier Bold is a powerful take on the original Red Frontier. With bourbon, triple sec, fresh lemon, and grenadine, it delivers a deeper, more intense whiskey experience.

Ingredients

  • 5 cl Bourbon or Rye Whiskey
  • 2 cl Triple Sec (e.g. Cointreau)
  • 2 cl Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1 cl Grenadine
  • Crushed Ice
  • Lemon Zest (garnish)

Preparation

  1. Shake all ingredients with ice.
  2. Strain into a glass filled with crushed ice.
  3. Garnish with lemon zest.

Flavor Profile

Intense, citrusy, with bold whiskey notes and a balanced sweetness. Perfect for experienced cocktail fans.

Strength

Strength: 7/10 – Strong whiskey-based cocktail

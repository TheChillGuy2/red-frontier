Red Frontier – Signature Cocktail

Red Frontier is a modern, ruby-red signature cocktail that combines the warmth of whiskey with the refreshing brightness of cranberry juice, cola, grenadine, and fresh mint. Served over crushed ice and garnished with mint, it's a bold yet approachable drink perfect for summer evenings or a signature bar menu.

Ingredients

  • 4 cl Whiskey
  • 6 cl Cranberry Juice
  • 4 cl Cola
  • 1 cl Grenadine
  • Crushed Ice
  • Fresh Mint (garnish)

Preparation

  1. Fill a tumbler glass with crushed ice.
  2. Add whiskey, cranberry juice and cola.
  3. Pour in grenadine slowly to create a color gradient.
  4. Stir gently and garnish with fresh mint.

Flavor Profile

Aromatic, slightly sweet, smooth, and refreshing with a cooling mint finish. Perfect for both casual and formal settings.

Strength

Strength: 5/10 – Balanced and approachable

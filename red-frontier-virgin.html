Red Frontier Virgin – Non-Alcoholic Cocktail

Red Frontier Virgin is a refreshing, alcohol-free interpretation of the classic Red Frontier. With cranberry juice, cola, grenadine, lime, and mint, it's a perfect summer drink for all ages.

Ingredients

  • 6 cl Cranberry Juice
  • 4 cl Cola
  • 1 cl Grenadine
  • 1 cl Fresh Lime Juice
  • Crushed Ice
  • Fresh Mint (garnish)

Preparation

  1. Fill a glass with crushed ice.
  2. Add cranberry juice, cola, grenadine, and lime juice.
  3. Stir gently.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint.

Flavor Profile

Fruity, sweet-tart, and refreshing with a citrus twist. Ideal as a mocktail or daytime refreshment.

Strength

Strength: 0/10 – Alcohol-free
